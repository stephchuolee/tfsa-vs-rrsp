How did you get these numbers?

After Tax Deposit: A TFSA allows you to invest your after-tax money. With an RRSP, an after-tax deposit of means your initial deposit will be . This is determined by your current income's tax rate.

Future Value: This is the value of your invesment (in today's dollars) in years. This is determined by your investment period, the return on investment, and the expected rate of inflation for each year.

Tax Paid Upon Withdrawal: This is determined by your expected retirement tax rate. This only applies to your RRSP because you already paid taxes on your TFSA deposit amount.

After Tax Future Value: The difference between the future value of your investment and the tax paid upon withdrawal.
